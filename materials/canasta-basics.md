# Canasta Basics

## Goal

Score more points than the opposing team by making melds (sets of cards with the same rank), completing canastas, and going out.

## Setup

- 4 players in 2 teams.
- Use two standard 52-card decks plus all jokers (108 cards total).
- Each player is dealt 11 cards.
- One discard pile and one stock pile are formed.

## Turn Summary

1. Draw from the stock or (when allowed) pick up the discard pile.
2. Meld cards from your hand (optional, subject to meld requirements).
3. Discard one card to end your turn.

## Canasta Types

- **Natural canasta**: 7 cards of the same rank with no wild cards.
- **Mixed canasta**: 7 cards of the same rank including wild cards.

## Ending a Hand

A player may go out after their team has at least one canasta and they can play all remaining cards (with one final discard unless going out concealed).

## Common Scoring Values

- Jokers: 50
- Twos (wild): 20
- Aces and 8-K: 10
- 4-7: 5
- Red 3: bonus (conditions apply)

> Rules vary by table. Treat this file as a starter reference and align with your group before play.
